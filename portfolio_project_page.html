INTRODUCTION

Welcome to my personal portfolio website! Here, I have created a platform to showcase my skills, experiences, work, and achievements in a visually appealing manner. This website serves as a comprehensive resource for my university assessors, potential employers, and clients, providing them with all the necessary information about who I am, what I can offer, and how to connect with me. Whether you are interested in learning more about my professional background or exploring my qualifications, this website is designed with you in mind.

MAIN GOAL

My main goal is to create a visually captivating and user-friendly personal portfolio website that effectively highlights my skills, experiences, and achievements. By showcasing my expertise and professionalism, I aim to attract new clients, employers, and job opportunities while establishing a strong online presence.


Used method

For our project, we've decided to utilize the Agile methodology. This means that we'll be following several sprints (in this case 4 sprints), moving through the phases in the exact order they're listed. Initially, I will gather and carefully analyze all the requirements for the project. After that, we'll proceed to designing and content, development and testing, and finally launch and maintenance.
